Hi! I'm Nguyen

Who I am

I am a person who is enthusiastic about human interactions and how we can work together to make a better impact on our communities, locally and hopefully globally. Currently, I am studying Computer Science at UC San Diego, one of the best public colleges in the U.S. (and has the nicest beaches in California).

I lived in Viet Nam for 20 years before I moved to the U.S. and it is very interesting to see the differences in every aspects of life between here and my home country. With my background, I hope that I can bring a new perspective in creating products that understand and represent cultural diversity so that we can all work towards a common goal without getting hinder by complicated tools and medium.

Viet Nam is a little tropical country in South East Asia. In the words of Anthony Bourdain, your life will change once you visit this country. Also, we have the BEST food.

What I am interested in

  • Find (good) solution(s) to problems
  • Work with people who share similar goals & values
  • Learn about human communications, interactions, and behaviors
  • Cook new dishes without recipes

What I want to be & what I want to do in my life

  • Work with projects that can better the life of other people, especially those in developing countries.
  • Be able to bring academic opportunities to children in areas that does not have access to education.
  • Work with NGOs that focus on providing education and jobs opportunites for underprivileged community.

Why I want to do all these things

I believe in empathy. I want to believe that good things exist in life despite all the negatives that have been spiralling down recently. Even if the world is not a nice place to live right now, I want to have the ability to make it better for myself, for my community, and for future generations.

How will I get to where I want?

Good things take time. I am still practicing my craft and learning through classes and personal projects. I don't know what I don't know, but I make a pledge to myself that I will try my best to pursue knowledge. One step at a time I will be able to identify the things that I don't know, and ultimately, I will understand them and they will become my personal assets.
